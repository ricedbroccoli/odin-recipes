Recipe for Steak

A good simple steak is a classic dish that everyone should be able to make, and I will tell you how

  • Ingredients
  • Your Favorite Cut of Steak, mine is a 12oz Sirloin
  • 1/2 Stick of Butter
  • 1 Twig of thyme
  • 1 tbsp of Minced Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Raise a skillet to medium heat
  2. Add in the 1/2 Stick of Butter
  3. Wait for Butter to Melt, and Evenly Coat the Skillet
  4. While the Skillet is heating up, Go Ahead and put Salt and Pepper to Taste on the Steak
  5. As soon as the butter is bubbling, put the Steak Offset from the Middle of the Skillet Towards You
  6. In the Space Inbetween You and the Steak, put the Thyme and Garlic
  7. Tilt the Skillet Towards you, so that the Butter Pools around the Thyme and Garlic
  8. Take a Spoon and use it to Throw the Butter onto the Steak. This is a Technique known as Basting
  9. Every 1 Minute, Flip the Steak Until it Reaches the Desired Internal Temperature
  10. Once the Steak is Finished Cooking, Let the Steak Rest for 5 to 10 Minutes
  11. Enjoy
