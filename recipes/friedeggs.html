Over Easy Fried Eggs

Over Easy Fried Eggs are an Incredibly Simple, Quick, Easy, and Most Importantly, Delicious Breakfast. They are Fried on the Outside, but Have a Runny Yolk that Toast Tastes Great Dipped in

  • The Amount of Eggs you Want
  • Enough Butter to Coat your Skillet
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a Skillet to Medium Heat
  2. Put the Butter in the Skillet, and Coat the Skillet Evenly
  3. Once the Butter is Bubbling, Crack an Egg into the Skillet
  4. Put Salt and Pepper on the Uncooked Egg Now
  5. Wait Till the Bottom White of the Egg Starts Bubbling, Then Flip
  6. Let it Cook for about 45 Seconds, then Plate
  7. Enjoy!
